A forward contract is a contract to purchase something at a point in the future. If payment is made at the time of the contract, this is called a prepaid forward. For a share of stock, the price of a prepaid forward is the current stock price minus the present value of the dividends that will be issued prior to delivery. This is an adjustment due to the fact that the buyer does not get any of the dividends issued prior to delivery of the stock.
