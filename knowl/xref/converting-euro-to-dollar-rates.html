A U.S. investor purchased a 9-month European bond denominated in Euros. The bond had a face value of 1000 euros, and was priced to yield an effective annual yield of 0.96% (European). The investor purchased the bond on January 1, 2020, when the exchange rate was 1.11 US Dollars per Euro. Nine months later, on October 1, 2020, when the bond matured, the exchange rate was 1.17 US dollars per Euro. Determine the US effective annual yield earned by the investor.
