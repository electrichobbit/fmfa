    8.

    🔗
    Suppose that the continuously compounded annual risk-free interest rate is \(r = 3\%\text{.}\) A bank takes the risk-free rate as its compensation for deferred consumption.
    🔗
    The data the bank has shows that similar loans made to borrowers with similar credit risks default during the construction phase 5% of the time, and never default after the construction phase. In the event of default, can recover 150,000 net of costs through foreclosure.     🔗
    The bank also pays 1.25% for inflation protection.

    If inflation in years 1, 2, and 3 is 2.3%, 1.9%, and 2.5% respectively, find the interest rate \(R\) paid by the borrower in each of the first three years, along with the amount of the annual payment.
